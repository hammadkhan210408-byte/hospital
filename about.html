Background History

Our hospital was established in 2010 with the aim of providing high-quality, affordable, and reliable medical services to the community. It was founded by Dr. Ahmed Khan, who started this institution with a strong commitment to ensure that every patient receives modern and safe treatment, regardless of their background or financial situation. In the beginning, the hospital operated with limited departments and a small medical team. Over the years, through dedication, trust, and continuous improvement, our hospital has grown into a well-established healthcare center offering multiple specialties, advanced diagnostic facilities, and a highly experienced staff committed to patient care.

Hospital Facilities

Our hospital is equipped with a wide range of modern medical facilities designed to ensure safe, efficient, and high-quality treatment for every patient. We continuously upgrade our equipment, improve our departments, and maintain strict healthcare standards to deliver the best possible experience.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Our ICU is fully equipped with advanced monitoring systems, ventilators, and emergency care devices to support patients with critical conditions. A team of experienced specialists is available round-the-clock to ensure immediate and effective treatment.

24/7 Emergency Department

Our Emergency Department operates 24 hours a day, providing urgent medical care for accidents, injuries, and life-threatening situations. Trained emergency staff ensures quick response and immediate treatment.

Operation Theatre (OT)

We have modern and fully sterilized operation theatres featuring advanced surgical instruments. Our expert surgeons perform a wide range of major and minor surgeries with precision and complete safety protocols.

Pharmacy

The hospital pharmacy provides genuine and approved medicines at affordable prices. It is managed by trained pharmacists to help patients receive the correct medication as prescribed by the doctors.
